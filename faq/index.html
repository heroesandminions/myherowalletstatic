What are the APY interest rates that are displayed on my dashboard? How do I earn these rates?

APY means Annual Percentage Yield, and it is the total amount you can make in a year (including the compounding in your principal amount). We
compound your principal amount at the end of a weekly payout or a fixed deposit payout.
So to make the maximum yield on your funds in one year, it’s very simple:
– Lock all your funds in 1 month Fixed Deposits
– Renew the FIxed Deposits every time they mature
At the end of the year, you would have made the listed APY rates
